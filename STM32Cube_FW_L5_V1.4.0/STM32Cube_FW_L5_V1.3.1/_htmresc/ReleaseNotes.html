STM32CubeL5 Firmware Package V1.3.0 / 26-June-2020


Fourth official release of STM32CubeL5 Firmware
  • CMSIS 5.6.0 (updated DSP libraries (moved to CMSIS/DSP/Lib))
  • Corrections and enhancement of CMSIS Device, HAL and Low Layer drivers
  • Middleware updates: FreeRTOS, USB Device, USB Power Delivery (Core & Device), MbedTLS, Mbed crypto, trustedfirmware and mcuboot (new)
  • TFM/SBSFU applications updates (crypto scheme with FW encryption, single image and external flash support)
  • Several corrections applied in project examples generated from STM32CubeMX
